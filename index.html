About Kalyani Mali

Kalyani Mali is a UI/UX Product Designer based in Nashik, India, with over 2 years of experience in designing user-centered digital products, mobile applications, and branding systems. She specializes in wireframing, prototyping, UI design systems, and creating scalable digital experiences using tools like Figma and modern frontend technologies.

Founder of LocalConnect

Kalyani Mali is also the Founder of LocalConnect, an AI-powered hyperlocal services platform that connects users with trusted local service providers. LocalConnect focuses on trust, transparency, and smart service discovery using AI-driven systems.

UI Design, UX Design, Product Design, Design Systems, Wireframing, Prototyping, User Research, Information Architecture, Branding, Figma, Adobe XD, Frontend Development.
